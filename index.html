Travel

Please aim to arrive by 12:30pm and aim to be seated in The Barn at 12:45pm at the latest.

Train

The nearest main train station is Guildford Station. If coming from London, you will need to catch the train from London Waterloo to Guildford. It is a 45 min journey. The drive from the station to the venue is 17 minutes. If you are planning to travel to Guildford, please let us know as we are arranging a shuttle service from Guildford Station to Gate Street Barn, should you need it.

Car

The venue address is: Gate St, Bramley, Guildford GU5 0LR. It is a rural location on a private estate close to international airports, mainline rail, the A3 and M25. Please do not rely solely on SatNav - many systems take you on a circuitous route! Although the postal address is Bramley, the turning off the A281 is approx 2 miles south of Bramley heading towards Horsham.
